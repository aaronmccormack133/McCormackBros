Welcome!

Welcome to McCormackBrosRail.ie. We are an Irish business with a focus on rail maintenance supplying management, site clearance and woodland management services for Irish Rail. We also offer a wide range on rail plant services to the Rail Network in Ireland. The company also has a long established agricultural contracting business with a full range of services available to the farming community.

McCormack Bros Rail are an experienced road rail plant supplier in Ireland. Our staff have an extensive skill base delivering rail plant services from the following areas;

  • Railway Operations
  • Vegetation Management
  • Tree Surgery
  • Tree Management
  • Drainage Services
  • Training Services

McCormack Bros Rail provide an extensive, high quality agricultural contracting service to the farming community. We provide a wide range of machinery spanning all the needs and requirements of the modern farmer. Our team is highly professional and experienced and can offer expertise in the following areas;

  • Silage Harvesting
  • Bail Silage
  • Slurry Spreading
  • Ploughing and Reseeding
  • Land Drainage
  • Hedge-Cutting
  • Tree Surgery
  • Muck Spreading
  • Fertiliser Spreading
  • Crop Spraying
